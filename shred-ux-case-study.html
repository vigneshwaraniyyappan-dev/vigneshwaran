Overview

Project Background

Shred is a peer-to-peer student marketplace that helps university students find, borrow, lend, or sell items they need — from textbooks to lab equipment to everyday essentials. The platform creates a safe, community-driven economy within a campus environment.

The core challenge was designing a trustworthy, frictionless experience across iOS, Android, and web that serves both students who need items and those who want to monetise things they already own.

🔍

Problem

Students struggle to find affordable access to items they need short-term and have no safe platform to lend or sell within their campus community.

🎯

Goal

Design a cross-platform app with trustworthy user profiles, listings, messaging, and transaction tracking — built specifically for student needs.

📐

Scope

End-to-end UX: research, information architecture, user flow, and low-fidelity wireframes for all three platforms.

User Research

Understanding the Users

Research consisted of interviews with 12 students across undergraduate and postgraduate levels, supplemented by a survey of 85 students. Key themes emerged around trust, convenience, and price sensitivity.

01

Trust is the #1 barrier

78% of students said they would not borrow from a stranger without a verified profile, rating system, or mutual social connection on campus.

02

Search and discovery is broken

Students use WhatsApp group chats, Facebook Marketplace, and word of mouth to find items — no dedicated student-focused solution exists at most institutions.

03

Messaging friction causes drop-off

65% of students abandoned a potential transaction because messaging was slow, unclear, or they didn't know how to arrange payment safely.

04

Mobile-first, with web for posting

Browsing and messaging happen on mobile; uploading item photos and writing detailed listings tends to happen on desktop or laptop.

Recommendations

Design Principles & Next Steps

Based on research findings, four priority design recommendations emerged for the Shred platform.

High Priority

Verified Student Profiles

Require university email verification and display a "Verified Student" badge. Pair with a rating system visible on every listing card and profile page.

High Priority

In-App Transaction Tracking

Build a dedicated transaction log so both parties can track borrow periods, return deadlines, and payment status without relying on memory or external messaging.

Medium Priority

Smart Notifications

Send timely nudges for return deadlines, new messages, and listings matching saved searches. Avoid over-notification — let users set their own preferences.

Medium Priority

Onboarding Trust Flow

Guide new users through profile setup, email verification, and their first listing or browse in a 3-step onboarding to maximise activation and early trust signals.
