Hi, I'm Vigneshwaran — a UI/UX Designer based in Coimbatore with over 3 years of experience turning complex problems into clean, intuitive digital experiences.

I believe good design is invisible. When a product works so smoothly that users accomplish their goals without thinking about the interface, that's when design has done its job. I approach every project with deep curiosity about the people who will use it — what they need, what frustrates them, and what would genuinely delight them.

My process combines structured UX methods (user interviews, competitive audits, information architecture) with strong visual craft — building pixel-perfect Figma designs, interactive prototypes, and reusable design systems that developers love to implement.
